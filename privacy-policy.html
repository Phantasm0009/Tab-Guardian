Tab Guardian Privacy Policy

Overview

Tab Guardian is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and what choices you have regarding your data when using our browser extension.

Information Collection and Use

Tab Guardian is designed with privacy in mind. Here's how we handle your information:

Local Storage Only

All data used by Tab Guardian is stored exclusively on your device using Chrome's storage APIs. This includes:

  • Your password (securely hashed)
  • List of locked tabs and domains
  • Tab activity timestamps
  • Recently closed tabs in the Tab Vault
  • Configuration preferences

No External Servers

Tab Guardian does not transmit any of your data to external servers. There are:

  • No analytics or tracking
  • No data collection
  • No cloud storage of your tabs or browsing history
  • No third-party services utilized

Required Permissions

Tab Guardian requires several browser permissions to function properly:

tabs

Used to access and manage your browser tabs, including locking, monitoring activity, and applying privacy protections.

storage

Used to save your preferences, password, and tab history locally on your device.

alarms

Used to schedule automatic tab decluttering and periodic security scans.

scripting

Used to detect form data before decluttering tabs and implement stealth mode visual changes.

notifications

Used to alert you about decluttered tabs and provide undo options.

host permissions

Tab Guardian requests "<all_urls>" permission to be able to apply locking and privacy protections on any website you choose to protect.

Data Security

Your password is secured using the SHA-256 hashing algorithm, ensuring it is never stored as plain text. All local data is protected by your browser's built-in security measures.

Data Retention

Tab activity data and the Tab Vault contents are stored only for the duration you specify in your settings. You can clear this data at any time through the extension's options page.

Your Choices

You have full control over your data:

  • You can clear your Tab Vault history at any time
  • You can modify how long tab history is retained
  • You can disable any feature that you don't want to use
  • You can uninstall the extension to remove all stored data

Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy if there are changes to how the extension functions. Significant changes will be communicated through the extension's update notes.
